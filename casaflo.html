CasaFlo

A unified booking platform for short-lets and rentals.

Overview

The Lagos short-let market is crowded and fast-moving. CasaFlo had built trust through word of mouth and manual bookings, but growth meant their offer needed to be clearer, faster, and available on demand. Beyond short-lets, they wanted to bundle cars, beach houses, hotels, and essentials into one confident experience.

Problem

Bookings ran on calls, DMs, and spreadsheets. Agents hunted for availability, customers waited for callbacks, and payment confirmations took too long. CasaFlo needed a single web and mobile platform where listings, booking, and payments felt instant, trustworthy, and self-serve.

Task

Design a product that lets people discover spaces, compare options, and complete bookings without calling an agent—while keeping the brand’s personal, premium feel.

Responsibilities

  • Craft a landing narrative that clarifies value and reduces bounce
  • Design the web booking experience end-to-end
  • Translate the flow to mobile with parity and speed
  • Define a consistent visual system for the extended offer
  • Minimize friction across search, booking, and payment

User Flow

Based on client goals and competitive reviews, I mapped the journeys to keep people oriented: search → compare → book → pay → confirm. The flow trims agent dependency and makes “what happens next” obvious on every step.

Wireframe

I condensed the flow into four core screens—landing, listings, booking, payment—to validate layout and copy quickly with the client before investing in high fidelity.

Landing page

Hero copy focuses on speed and trust, with quick filters and proof points that help visitors jump straight into relevant stays.

Mobile App Screens

Conclusion

COVID paused development, but the validated designs defined clear success criteria and a roadmap the team can resume when ready.

  • Shift 80% of bookings to self-serve web and app
  • Cut time-to-booking by simplifying the decision path
  • Retain 50% of first-time visitors through clearer value and proof
  • Increase sign-ups by 35% via streamlined onboarding
  • Drive 40% wallet adoption to speed repeat purchases
  • Reach 50% app adoption among active customers
