About me

I’m Samuel Omidiji, a multidisciplinary designer and creative technologist focused on turning complex ideas into clear, interactive, and scalable digital experiences. I blend UX design, visual storytelling, and rapid prototyping to explore new interfaces across web, mobile, AI, and emerging tech. My work spans creative coding, mixed reality concepts, and experimental tools that push how people interact with technology. I move fast from idea to prototype, collaborating tightly with product and engineering to bring bold ideas to life.

Toolbox: Figma, HTML/CSS/JS, P5.js, Webflow, Adobe Creative Suite, Unity, and AI tools.
