CasaFlo

Overview

The realtor business in Lagos is on with many competitors, and to stand out, your proposition and offer should be very attractive. Casaflo, a realtor, who has been in business for quite a while wants to create and automate their business as well as adding other packages. Dominantly dealing in leasing out short-lets, Casaflo aims to add more leasing options for vehicle, beach houses, hotels and other essentials.

Problem

Bookings and operations at Casaflo are done manually as customers rely on agents to do the bookings or contacting Casaflo through mobile calls or social media handle. As part of the expansion, Casaflo wants to create a platform that would have all their listings as well as making booking and payment seamless.
The platform would be on web and mobile.

Task

My task was to develop the product by designing a user interface that would allow users navigate the product easily and make decision to book a short-let seamlessly.

Responsibilities
  • Design a landing page
  • Develop a web app interface
  • Create the mobile app interface
  • Maintain brand colors.
  • Ensure the UX is seamless.
